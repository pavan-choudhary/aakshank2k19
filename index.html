Introduction.

A four day surfeit of fun activities and ecstasy,
"Aakshank" , central India's mega and fantabulous event is back again with, more plus extra, electrifying and vivifying events.

Our fest covers diverse events ranging from competitions like Dance, Drama, Fashion show, Singing to Star nights, DJ Nights, Informals and Stuntmania.

Till now "Aakshank" had the opportunity to host stars like Papon, Nikhil D'souza, Mohammed Irfan, Nikhil Chinappa, DJ willy, Progressive Brothers.Hoping to make this list a never ending one and with this we're stepping into the brand new 2k19 chapter.

Having blessed to be a part of the distinguished cultural bonanza, we, students of IET invite you all to our event.
Join us and together we'll make "Aakshank" the most gratifying and memorable 4 days of the year 2019.
